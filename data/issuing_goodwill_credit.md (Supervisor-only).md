Issuing Goodwill Credit Procedure (Supervisor Level)

إجراءات إصدار goodwill credit (للـ supervisors بس)

يا فندم، الـ goodwill credit بنستخدمه عشان نحافظ على العملاء المهمين أو في حال SLA breach.

القواعد:
- Max 20% of monthly bill for regular customers.
- Up to 50% for VIP / Golden customers.
- Must have valid ticket (P1 or P2) with proper documentation.
- Approval chain: Supervisor → Billing team → OSS credit note.

Steps:
1. Review the ticket history and customer sentiment.
2. Calculate the credit amount based on impact duration.
3. Apply the credit in BSS and inform customer: "يا فندم، وافقنا على credit قدره X جنيه على الفاتورة الجاية."

Audit Note: كل credit يتسجل للـ review الشهري.
